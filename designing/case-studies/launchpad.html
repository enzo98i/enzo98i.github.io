Amazon Launchpad

Discovery, Redefined

Amazon Launchpad is a curated marketplace for innovative products made by emerging brands and startups. Launchpad works closely with individual inventors and innovative startups providing resources and opportunities to get products to market. In 2019, Launchpad merged with Amazon Exclusives which focused on products offered exclusively through Amazon. In 2020, awareness of Launchpad remained low. This is where we came in.

Services

Content Creative
Creative Strategy
Video Production

A New Frontier

Amazon approached us to elevate the creative around one of their most unique business units. Being the only consumer-facing section of Amazon that sells products across every major category, Launchpad did not fit into existing category strategy, messaging, or creative. With an inventor/start-up (think Kickstarter or Product Hunt) focused model, we were faced with a challenge of creating a unique and distinct brand and visual voice that still lived within the Amazon ecosystem.

Building Worlds

Working closely with the a multi-disciplinary creative and client team we crafted a pop-art infused creative treatment that inspired the creation of individual worlds connected through colorstory around each product category. With a strategy focused on a younger and more tech-savvy audience our goal was to create beautiful, engaging, artistic, and sophisticated spaces that highlighted the many innovative and unconventional products sold on Launchpad.

Impact

The result? Beautiful, immersive worlds, spanning 6 product categories and more than 50+ products featured. Our production strategy allowed us to provide a wide spectrum of content for omni-channel distribution: long-form video, short-form social video, gifs, and static photography.To date, the video content has received over 3 million cumulated views.

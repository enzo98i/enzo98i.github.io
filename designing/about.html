Michael is a creative connector, brand whisperer, and community champion who is driven by his obsessive curiosity. Raised by his local hip-hop scene, he’s traveled the world battling in breakdance competitions and managing artists. He translated these experiences into his work as a brand manager for Red Bull and later on at global agencies such as Razorfish and Dentsu. He founded Milli in 2014 during the first boom of the social media era seeing an opportunity to leverage new tools and norms to help more than just big brands but small businesses, changemakers, creative industry, and emerging talent. In 2023 he became a founding film commissioner for the city of Seattle.
